# Privacy Policy for Koa Sleep

**Last Updated:** November 14, 2025

This privacy policy ("Policy") describes how Koa Sleep ("we," "us," or "our") collects, uses, and shares information in connection with your use of our web application.

## 1. Information We Collect

When you connect your Fitbit account to Koa Sleep, we request access to the following data:

* **Sleep Data:** We collect your sleep logs (start time, end time, sleep stages, etc.) to generate the data visualizations on your dashboard.
* **Profile Data:** We may collect basic profile information (like your user ID and timezone) to properly associate data with your account and display times correctly.
* **Access Tokens:** We collect and store your Fitbit OAuth 2.0 access and refresh tokens to make API calls on your behalf. These are stored securely.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for one purpose:

* **To provide the service:** Your sleep data is used to render your personal sleep dashboard. We do not analyze your data for any other purpose, and no human will look at your data unless required for troubleshooting at your request.

## 3. How We Share Your Information

We do not and will not ever sell, rent, or share your personal data or sleep data with any third party.

## 4. Data Storage and Security

Your Fitbit data and tokens are stored in our application database. We take reasonable measures to protect your information from unauthorized access.

## 5. Revoking Your Access

You may revoke Koa Sleep's access to your Fitbit data at any time by going to your Fitbit account settings.
